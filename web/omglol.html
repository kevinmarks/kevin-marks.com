Routing kevinmarks.org to omg.lol

  • step 1: make an A record in DNS at hover.com for 5.78.24.5
  • step 2: set nameservers for the domain at hover to ns1.hover.com, ns2.hover.com
  • step 3: set up the omg.lol switchboard to point to kevinmarks.org as well
  • step 4: wait 15 minutes for DNS
