Plastech Innovation:

Climate Change is one of the biggest crises facing humanity, and he plastic crisis is harming sea animals

we take post-consumer plastic that is currently landfilled or incinerated - we convert it into aggregate for concrete users

concrete is responsible for 5% of global CO₂ production - we can reduce this with our lighter aggregate

we can look at things on the molecular level and incorporate more of our aggregate into concrete

we're working with Biffa - we get paid for our raw material as thy are charged £120/ton for plastic now

we are testing percentages but hope for about 20%
